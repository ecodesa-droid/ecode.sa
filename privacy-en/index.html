Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

This policy was prepared to ensure Electronic Code Company – eCode's commitment to data protection standards and to regulate processing of information provided by clients and website users.

Your use of our website or services means your agreement to all terms mentioned in this policy.

First: Policy Scope and Application

This policy applies to all data that eCode collects, processes, or stores, whether when using the company website, electronic forms, or direct communication via email, WhatsApp, or any other communication method.

Policy includes:

  • Clients
  • Visitors
  • Service request applicants
  • Individuals appearing in images or materials photographed within projects

Second: Data We Collect

1. Personal Data Provided Directly by Client

When filling forms or requesting service, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email
  • Business or project data
  • Uploaded documents
  • Images, videos, and files required for work execution
  • Client location (optional)

2. Technical Data Collected Automatically

When accessing website, technical data is collected to improve performance:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Visit time and date
  • Pages visited
  • Analytics data
  • Cookies

3. Photography and Media Production Data

If client requests photography or content production service, data may include:

  • People images
  • Videos
  • Audio recordings
  • Photography locations
  • Creative session data

Important note: Use of these materials is limited to the agreed purpose within project only, and eCode has no right to publish or use them in other works without written client consent.

Third: How We Use Data

eCode uses data for following purposes:

  • Executing requests and projects
  • Communicating with client regarding project
  • Sending invoices and updates
  • Providing technical support or follow-up
  • Improving service quality
  • Maintaining records and documents
  • Protecting company rights from misuse

We do not use data for any marketing purposes without explicit client consent.

Fourth: Data Sharing with Third Parties

We may share some data with following parties as needed:

  • Collaborators and freelancers working on project execution
  • Hosting and cloud service providers
  • Payment system providers
  • Legal or government entities upon request

All parties are committed to data confidentiality and necessary use limits only.

Fifth: Data Storage and Protection

eCode stores data securely, and uses:

  • File encryption
  • Access restriction to authorized only
  • Periodic backup copies

And takes all necessary measures according to Personal Data Protection System in Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Sixth: Data-Related Rights

Client has right to:

  • Request access to their data
  • Request data modification
  • Request data deletion (considering ongoing project commitments)
  • Withdraw consent for future data processing

Response is made within no more than 30 days.

Seventh: Data Deletion

eCode deletes client data after:

  • Project completion + 6 months

Unless regulations or contracts stipulate longer period.

Eighth: External Links

Our website may contain links to external parties.

eCode is not responsible for content, security, or policies of those sites.

Ninth: Policy Amendments

eCode has right to amend this policy at any time.

Your continued use of website is considered consent to amendments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How is payment done?
A non-refundable deposit is paid upon proposal approval, then payments are completed according to phases or before final delivery as agreed.
How long does project execution take?
It varies according to service type and content volume, and an estimated timeline is mentioned in the proposal. Any delay in responses or material provision from client affects the timeline.
Can I get a refund after starting?
The deposit reserves team time and effort, so it's non-refundable after work begins, as stated in Terms & Conditions.
Do I receive editable files?
Final publication-ready files are delivered, and editable files can be delivered for additional fees agreed upon in advance.
